VultureSMP is a private Vanilla Survival Minecraft server. This does not however mean you will never be able to join. Occasionally, we may be accepting new members, the best way to keep track of this is to follow @VultureSMP on Twitter. Currently, our small community consists of 13 members.

VultureSMP is owned and maintained by Ben_Andy. He started the server on October 28, 2014, and it is still going strong, with an amazing community!

If you feel so inclined feel free to contact us!

We also have several members hosting lets plays on their personal YouTube channel, so for some action from the server, be sure to check the playlist!
